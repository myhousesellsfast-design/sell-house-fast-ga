# Sell Your House Decision Framework (Georgia 2026)

If you're unsure how to sell your house, this guide will help you choose the best option based on your situation.

---

## Option 1: Traditional Sale (Real Estate Agent)

Best if:
- You have time (60–120+ days)
- The home is in good condition
- You want to try for market price

Pros:
- Potentially higher price

Cons:
- Repairs often needed
- Commissions and fees
- Longer timeline

---

## Option 2: Cash Sale (Fast Sale)

Best if:
- You need to sell quickly
- The home needs repairs
- You want certainty

Pros:
- Fast closing (7–14 days)
- No repairs needed
- No commissions

Cons:
- May be slightly below retail price

---

## Option 3: Hold or Rent Property

Best if:
- You want long-term income
- You can manage tenants
- You don’t need immediate cash

Cons:
- Maintenance responsibility
- Risk of vacancies or repairs

---

## Simple Decision Rule:

- Need speed → Cash sale  
- Want max price → Agent listing  
- Want income → Rent property
