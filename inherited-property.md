# Selling an Inherited House in Georgia (2026 Guide)

If you inherited a house in Georgia, you may be dealing with repairs, probate, or unwanted responsibilities.

## Common Challenges:
- The home needs repairs
- Multiple family members involved
- Ongoing taxes and insurance
- Probate process

## Your Options:
- Keep the property
- Rent it out
- Sell with a real estate agent
- Sell the house fast for cash

## Why Many People Choose Cash Sale:
- No repairs needed
- Fast closing
- Avoid ongoing costs
- Simple process
