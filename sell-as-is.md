# Sell Your House As-Is in Georgia (No Repairs Needed)

If your house needs repairs, updates, or cleanup, you can still sell it without fixing anything.

## What “As-Is” Means:
Selling as-is means:
- No repairs required
- No renovations needed
- No cleaning or staging

## Common Situations:
- House needs major repairs
- Inherited property
- Tired landlord property
- Vacant or damaged home

## Your Options:
- List with an agent (repairs usually needed)
- Sell as-is to a cash buyer (fastest option)

## Why Cash Buyers Work:
- Buy in any condition
- Fast closing (7–14 days)
- No commissions or fees
