# Home Selling Timeline in Georgia (2026 Guide)

Understanding the timeline of selling a house helps you choose the best option for your situation.

---

## Traditional Sale Timeline (Agent Listing)
- Prep & repairs: 2–8 weeks  
- Listing period: 30–90+ days  
- Inspections & closing: 30–60 days  

👉 Total time: 60–120+ days

---

## Cash Sale Timeline (Fast Option)
- Offer review: 24–48 hours  
- Closing: 7–14 days  

👉 Total time: 1–2 weeks

---

## Why Timeline Matters
The longer a house sits on the market:
- More holding costs (mortgage, taxes, utilities)
- More uncertainty
- More price reductions

---

## When Speed Is Important
Fast sales are often used when:
- Facing foreclosure
- Relocating quickly
- Dealing with inherited property
- Selling a home needing repairs
